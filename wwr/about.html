History

It started in 1990. It was founded by the brothers in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. They wanted to share their deep love for the outdoors, rapid waters, and nature with everyone.

Over the last few decades, the company has grown from a single small raft into a premier group. We now navigate over a dozen rivers safely every single season.
